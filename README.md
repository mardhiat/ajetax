## What it does

Calculate your Nigerian tax liability in real-time. Just enter your income and deductions, and see exactly what you owe (or get back) based on 2026 tax regulations.

**Key features:**
- Progressive tax bracket calculations (0% to 25%)
- Employment income, investments, and capital gains
- Automatic deduction caps (pension, NHF, rent)
- Prepaid tax tracking (PAYE, WHT)
- Monthly payment estimates

## Stack

React + TypeScript + Vite + Tailwind CSS
